## About Me

&emsp;Yo! My name is Alexis and this site was put together primarily as a project for myself. I originally started getting into this a long time ago honestly thanks to video games asking for macro’s or fixing mods. Also at a young age I began getting more into technology since my families background involves similar fields not necessarily hacking but things involving technology and engineering. I always was interested in science and learning how things work under the hood pursuing understanding in why not just how. I always found hacking cool since it seemed like a logic puzzle you had to solve which I personally found fun plus who wasn’t an edgy teenager y’know? I’ve fiddled with things in the past doing things such as soldering to fix a guitar, replacing parts on my PSP like a broken screen, and trying to revive old hardware to give it a fresh breathe of life. I’d say Anime being something I genuinely enjoy in my free time as well, any One Piece fans?  I really gotta thank .Hack series for sparking more interest to hacking as well. Before anyone reading this asks my favorites is Cowboy Bebop, Trigun, One Piece, Deadman Wonderland, and .Hack series.

&emsp;One of my favorite hobbies as well is gaming. I am extremely competitive and have hit some high ranks in a lot of games. Believe it or not I’ve been top 500 in overwatch 1 and 2, Gladiator in OG WoW, and even touched Global Elite in CS:GO but holy moly did that one burn me out with the cheaters I couldn’t bring myself to playing it again after that miserable grind. I’ve played a lot of other games as well RTS being my weakest but super fun for me and FPS being my strongest. Personally though I always was against hacking video games I just didn’t find interest in it at all. An example as to why would be like Pokemon if I used cheats to spawn a shiny starter then what stops me from just continuing that with with all my favorites like Snorlax, Gengar, and Sylveon? Plus personally gaming was always supposed to be an escape that was rewarding when overcoming difficulties like ranking up, completing a really difficult game, and getting something rare which I don’t want the satisfaction taken away from me. What interests me when it comes to hacking is systems, malware, and websites.

&emsp;Lastly I really wanted a place to host all my projects, labs, technical write-ups and research mostly in effort to gauge my own progress towards my goals as well as see my weaknesses.
